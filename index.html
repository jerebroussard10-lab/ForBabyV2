About the Artist

Painting the interior life of a face.

Taylor is a portrait artist whose practice lives at the intersection of psychology, intimacy, and oil paint. Every portrait begins with a question: what does this person carry in their eyes when no one is asking them to perform?

Their work is rich in both surface and subtext — layers of glazed oil that build depth the way memory builds meaning. Drawing on a lifelong fascination with the human face as a site of story, Taylor's paintings demand you stay.
